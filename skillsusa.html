Engineer For An Assistive Device

Skills: Inventor, Rapid Prototyping, Product Design

Project Overview

This project was a part of the 2022 SkillsUSA Engineering Design Competition. In this competition, teams of 3 were required to design a product and pitch it to a panel of industry professionals. Our product was a multi-function cosmetic stand that aims to help facilitate daily tasks such as applying makeup, shaving, and brushing teeth for amputees and quadraplegics. Our design won 1st place at regionals and 6th in states.

Design

Our design features an all-encompassing grip for various cylindrical objects. This is attached to a magnetic and flexible shaft that users can bend to suitable angles, yet still has enough retention for use in applying makeup or brushing teeth. This shaft is magnetically attached to a wooden base, that allows for it to be interchangeable with a limb guard alternative. The wooden base is a clamp that attaches to tables and flat surfaces, while the limb guard allows the device to be wrapped securely and comfortably around a limb if needed.
